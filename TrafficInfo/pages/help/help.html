NZ Traffic Usage Help

  • The maps roads are colour coded green, yellow, orange and red for fast to slow traffic respectively

  • You can use the search charm to navigate to a new location

  • Click on webcam to display a popup image of the camera

  • Tap on any part of the map to make the popups disappear

  • You can use the share charm to share a webcam image once a webcam has been selected

  • Use the settings to specify the refresh rate
